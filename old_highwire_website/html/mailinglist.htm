Mailing lists

Currently there are 2 mailing lists setup for the HighWire project, one aimed at the developers, allowing them to discuss and coordinate the continued work on the project. The other lists is aimed at the end users, providing them with a place where they can both have questions answered by HighWire contributors as well as share experiences with other HighWire users.
